A Cold Front is Coming

A cold front that moved through this late afternoon brought moderate valley rain and mountain rain/snow showers. Snow levels are sitting up 5,500ft today, as our low pressure moves east, we will see precipitation taper off by midnight tonight for all locations. Sunday will bring partly cloudy skies and isolated afternoon snow showers for higher elevations. More seasonal temperatures and partly to mostly cloudy skies are anticipated early next week.
