About Us

Since 1985, we’ve dedicated ourselves to offering people the best outdoor experiences of their lives. Whether you’re looking for a one-day or multi-day, beginner or someone who enjoys class V rapids, when it comes to adventure, some things always remain the same.

For most people, the experience starts the first time one of your friends or family members tells you about their unforgettable, white water trip. More than 60% of our travelers are repeat guests or were referred by a past adventurer. You’ll feel it the first time you pick up the phone and call one our friendly and knowledgeable adventure consultants. Everyone from our founder to riverguides, to staff on our team are genuinely invested in every guest, every trip and every lifetime memory. The journey is better together.
